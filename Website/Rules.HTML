Rules of the sport

At a very basic level, waterpolo is handball, only played in the water. Of course, there are hundreds of other rules and sub-rules, but thats the esccence. You can only ever touch the ball with one hand (unless you're the keeper), and you're not allowed touch the ground, again, unless you're the keeper, although as the pool is 2 meters or more deep, theres not much point for them anyway. You cannot push the ball under the water. You cannot grab another player. You cannot swim over anyone, unless they are holding the ball. You must wear a waterpolo hat. You cannot wear gogles.
