Join us!

To play water polo, you must be a confident swimmer, and you must be phisicaly fit. This is essential, as in game, it is an offence to touch the ground. If you are fit and a strong swimmer, please come along one day to try it out. Sign up is officialy at the start of september, but drop in to Aura, Drogheda, any day, and the staff will give you nesscary information to get into contact with one of the coaches.

As of right now, we only have four teams, the under 12's (mixed), the under 14's, (mixed), the under 16's, (men) and the senior team, (men). As of september 2012, however, we will hopefully have an under 16 girls team, and, numbers permitting, an all girl under 14 team too.
