About Us

To the left, you can see the national aquatic centre in Dublin. This is the home of Drogheda Waterpolo. Every tuesday evening, the senior and U-16 teams train here for 1 hour of pure waterpolo. We started training here in 2010, and since then, we have inproved greatly. We compete in the all-Irelands, the Leinster 1 league, and the Leinster 2 league. Our badge id the drogheda star and crescent. We have a International standard coach, and a national winning team. If you think you have what it takes to play, come down and try.
